# Code of Conduct

PeerNest should be welcoming, respectful, and useful for students and contributors.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Discuss ideas and code without personal attacks.
- Keep feedback specific and actionable.
- Respect privacy and do not share personal information without consent.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation.
- Publishing private information.
- Spam, trolling, or abusive comments.
- Attempts to exploit, disrupt, or compromise the project or its users.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block contributors, or take other reasonable actions to protect the project community.
